Lovely App for iPhone

Lovely app is not just another photo management app for your iPhone. It may not be the photos app you need. But it is the photos app you deserve.

Awesome features

Smart Categories

Have you ever wished you could find all the selfies in your iPhone? Or just all the screenshots? Lovely can find those photos for you. It can also find photos with faces, burst photos, and even categorize by shapes such as square, landscape, or portrait.

Create and View GIFs

If you find a funny GIF from the internet, don't hesitate to save it to your photos library. Lovely can play it. Oh, you can also select multiple photos from your library and create your own GIF!

Create Photostrips

They say a picture is worth a thousand words. So what will happen if you concatenate 2 or 3 or more photos? Will it be worth 2000, 3000 or infinity words?? :P With Lovely, you can easily make photostrips with customizable background color, spacing, and orientation.

More Awesome features

Advanced Photo Editing

Lovely is equipped with powerful editing tools. Some basic tools are Enhance, Effects (Filters), Crop and Rotation, Lighting, Color, Sharpness, Focus, Blemish, Blur, Whiten, and Redeye Removal. Some fun tools are Free Draw, Text Overlay, Stickers, and Meme!

View Photo's EXIF

Lovely exposes the metadata of your photos in detail for your pleasure, including Camera Model, Exposure Time, F Number, Focal Length, ISO Time, Dimension, and File Size.

Free Some Space

Don't have enough space to upgrade to the new and shiny iOS? Use Lovely to batch resize multiple photos or delete them.
